Happy Valley Roller Coaster - Inverse Proportional Function

A header for the site title for this item and the graph below.

Transformations：
\( \)
\( \)- When transforming the parent function \( f(x) = \frac{1}{x} \) to \( f(x) = \frac{-35}{x-7} - 10 \{ -5 < x < 5.3 \} \) , it is first vertically stretched by a factor of 35 and reflected over the x-axis, then horizontally translated 7 units to the right, and finally vertically translated 10 units downward with a domain restriction.
Domain and Range ：
\( \)-For the function \( f(x) = \frac{-35}{x-7} - 10 \) , its domain is \( (x \in \mathbb{R}, -5 < x < 5.3) \) , which is the interval specified in the function's definition; its range is \( (y \in \mathbb{R}, -7.08 < y < 10.59) \) , determined by the domain restricting the range of the reciprocal term.

Sunway Lagoon Slide - Cubic Polynomial Function

A header for the site title for this item and the graph below.

Transformations：
\(-\) When transforming the parent function \( f(x) = x^3 + x \) to \( f(x) = -0.4(x+1.6)^3 + 0.2(x+1.6) - 1 \) , it is first vertically stretched by a factor of 0.4 and reflected over the x-axis, then horizontally translated 1.6 units to the left, and finally vertically translated 1 unit downward.
Domain and Range ：
\(-\) For the function \( f(x) = -0.4(x+1.6)^3 + 0.2(x+1.6) - 1 \) , its domain is \( (x \in \mathbb{R}, -\infty < x < \infty) \) , as cubic functions (odd-degree polynomials) have no inherent domain constraints; its range is also \( (y \in \mathbb{R}, -\infty < y < \infty) \) , since cubic functions are unbounded, and this property remains unchanged after scaling and translation.

Russian-style Amusement Park Building - Tangent Function

A header for the site title for this item and the graph below.

Transformations：
\(-\) When transforming the parent function \( f(x) = \tan x \) to \( f(x) = 2.3 \tan(0.4x - 6.8) + 5.7 \left\{ -\frac{1}{2}\pi < x < \frac{1}{2}\pi \right\} \) , it is first horizontally stretched by a factor of 2.5, then horizontally translated 17 units to the right, next vertically stretched by a factor of 2.3, and finally vertically translated 5.7 units upward with a domain restriction.
Domain and Range ：
\(-\)For the function \( f(x) = 2.3\tan(0.4x - 6.8) + 5.7 \) , its domain is \( (x \in \mathbb{R}, -\frac{1}{2}\pi < x < \frac{1}{2}\pi) \) , which is the given interval (with none of the tangent function’s vertical asymptotes falling within this interval); its range is \( (y \in \mathbb{R}, -\infty < y < \infty) \) , as the tangent function can cover all real numbers, and this characteristic is not altered by scaling and translation.

DisneySea Volcano Structure - Exponential Function

A header for the site title for this item and the graph below.

Transformations：
\(-\) When transforming the parent function \( f(x) = 2^x \) to \( g(x) = 10 \cdot 2^{0.3(x + 3.1)} + 0.8 \{ -12 < x \} \) , it is first horizontally stretched by a factor of \( \frac{10}{3} \) , then horizontally translated 3.1 units to the left, next vertically stretched by a factor of 10, and finally vertically translated 0.8 units upward with a domain restriction; when transformed to \( h(x) = 10 \cdot 2^{0.3(-x + 3.1)} + 0.8 \{ 2 < x \} \) , it is horizontally reflected over the y-axis based on the transformation of \( g(x) \) and then the domain is adjusted.
Domain and Range ：
\(-\)For the function \( g(x) = 10 \cdot 2^{0.3(x+3.1)} + 0.8 \) , its domain is \( (x \in \mathbb{R}, -12 < x < \infty) \) , determined by the given lower bound; its range is \( (y \in \mathbb{R}, 2.35 < y < \infty) \) , since the exponential function \( 2^t > 0 \) and the exponential term has a lower bound within the domain. For the function \( h(x) = 10 \cdot 2^{0.3(-x+3.1)} + 0.8 \) , its domain is \( (x \in \mathbb{R}, 2 < x < \infty) \) , limited by the given lower bound; its range is \( (y \in \mathbb{R}, 0.8 < y < 13.40) \) , as the exponential term approaches 0 as \( x \) increases and has an upper bound within the domain.

Roller Coaster - Logarithmic Function

Transformations：
\(-\) When transforming the parent function \( f(x) = \log_2 x \) to \( f(x) = -1.5 \cdot \log_2(x + 0.1) - 1.7 \{ 0 < x \} \) , it is first vertically stretched by a factor of 1.5 and reflected over the x-axis, then horizontally translated 0.1 units to the left, and finally vertically translated 1.7 units downward with a domain restriction.

\(-\)For the function \( f(x) = -1.5\log_2(x + 0.1) - 1.7 \) , its domain is \( (x \in \mathbb{R}, 0 < x < \infty) \) —the argument of the logarithm \( x + 0.1 > 0 \) , and the domain is further restricted to \( x > 0 \) ; its range is \( (y \in \mathbb{R}, -\infty < y < 3.30) \) , adjusted by scaling and translation, as the value of the logarithmic function can increase to infinity.

Shanghai Disneyland Main Entrance - Square Root Function

Transformations：
\(-\) When transforming the parent function \( f(x) = \sqrt{x} \) to \( f(x) = 4.5\sqrt{0.3(x + 16)} + 7.2 \{ -16 < x \} \) , it is first horizontally stretched by a factor of \( \frac{10}{3} \) , then horizontally translated 16 units to the left, next vertically stretched by a factor of 4.5, and finally vertically translated 7.2 units upward with a domain restriction.
